Frequently asked questions

A few quick answers for visitors and users.

What is Calm Rhythm 2?

Calm Rhythm 2 is an app designed to help families keep a steadier daily rhythm with flexible routine blocks, gentle reminders, progress tracking, and calm visual feedback.

Who is it for?

The app is made for people who want a more organized and calmer way to manage daily routines around little ones.

How do I contact support?

Use the official support form or email maraholzer8@icloud.com.

Where can I read the privacy policy?

Visit the privacy page and open the official hosted privacy policy from there.

What kind of features are shown on this site?

The screenshots highlight onboarding, day planning, growth tracking, weekly summaries, family settings, and lifetime statistics.
